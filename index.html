Community Impact

A sneak peek at what we've done so far

Round one

With our first round of fundraising, here is what was accomplished:
- 700 bottles of water
- 300 packs of rice
- 250 cans of Tuna
- 90 units of organic boxed milk (and a few bags of dry milk)
- 36 pouches of baby food
- 24 cans of pre-made pasta
- 4 power inverters (to power small electronics from a car’s power adapter)
- 1 electric generator (still in transit)
